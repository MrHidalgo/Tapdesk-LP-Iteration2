Contacts management

Contacts provides a very efficient way for managing your clients and contacts while eliminating the need for a secondary contact manager. You can easily segment them into groups and perform even more robust actions specific to a group of customers in you database.

  • Easily import and export contacts in and out of your CRM.

  • You can build contact groups within the CRM or simply import them to categorize your customers accordingly.
